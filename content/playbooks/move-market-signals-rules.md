# Move Market Signals Rules

Market numbers come from public APIs or cache. The LLM interprets numbers only after retrieval.

Demand Pulse blends:

- Consumer sentiment or confidence
- Real disposable income
- Energy and utilities costs
- Personal savings rate
- Unemployment
- Discretionary versus staples ratio

Campaign Signal should translate market movement into inventory, sourcing, logistics, COGS, margin, and cash implications for $1M to $50M DTC physical goods brands.
