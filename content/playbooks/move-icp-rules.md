# Move ICP Rules

Strong-fit clients are DTC or ecommerce physical goods brands, usually $1M to $50M revenue, selling in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, or similar markets.

Positive signals:

- Physical product catalog
- Shopify, Amazon, retail, wholesale, subscription, or multi-channel sales
- Inventory, sourcing, logistics, NPD, 3PL, vendor, margin, or landed cost pain
- Lean team without enough supply chain leadership
- Identifiable founder, operator, ecommerce lead, or supply chain owner

Disqualifiers:

- Dropshipper
- Marketplace-only
- Too small
- Agency
- SaaS
- Info product
- Service business
- No physical goods
