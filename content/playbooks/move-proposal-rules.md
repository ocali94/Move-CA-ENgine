# Move Proposal Rules

Proposal Studio follows a seven-section sequence:

1. Cover and Context
2. What We Heard
3. Opportunity and Risk Map
4. Recommended Engagement Path
5. Scope Pillars
6. Timeline, Cadence, Team, and SLAs
7. Investment, Assumptions, and Next Steps

Each section must be approved before the next one is generated. Approved sections lock. If an earlier section is unlocked, later sections should be reviewed for consistency.

Current discovery facts outrank every other source. Past proposals influence structure, offer logic, phrasing patterns, and section flow only. They do not supply current client facts, outcomes, or pricing.
