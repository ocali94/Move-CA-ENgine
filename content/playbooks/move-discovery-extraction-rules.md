# Move Discovery Extraction Rules

Extract only facts supplied by discovery notes, intake answers, project fields, or selected references.

Capture:

- Company and website
- Contacts and roles
- Product category
- Revenue, SKU count, annual PO value
- Channels and warehouse setup
- Current suppliers and regions
- Pain points with evidence
- Repeated phrases
- Urgency, budget, and decision signals
- Desired outcomes
- Risks, constraints, and missing info
- Recommended service path with rationale

Do not invent revenue, pricing, savings, supplier performance, regions, or timelines.
